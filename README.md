# 🌸 Hormone Harmony

## Beyond Period Tracking: A PCOS-Aware Wellness & Behavioral Insights Platform

Hormone Harmony is a full-stack wellness platform designed specifically for women navigating PCOS/PCOD. Unlike traditional period-tracking applications that primarily focus on cycle dates and reminders, Hormone Harmony emphasizes awareness, education, symptom understanding, behavioral pattern tracking, and supportive wellness experiences.

The project was built around a simple observation: many women with PCOS experience irregular cycles, hormonal symptoms, lifestyle challenges, misinformation, and emotional uncertainty that are not adequately addressed by generic menstrual health applications.

Rather than attempting to provide medical diagnoses or unreliable health predictions, Hormone Harmony focuses on helping users understand their experiences through structured tracking, educational content, and personalized wellness resources.

---

## Why This Project Exists

Most menstrual health applications assume relatively regular cycle patterns.

PCOS often introduces:

* Irregular or absent ovulation
* Unpredictable cycle lengths
* Hormonal imbalance
* Acne, mood fluctuations, fatigue, and cravings
* Lifestyle-related symptom variability
* Widespread myths and misinformation
* Limited awareness among family members and support systems

Hormone Harmony was designed to address these challenges through a combination of behavioral tracking, PCOS-focused education, awareness resources, and user-centered wellness workflows.

## Key Features

### 📝 Symptom & Wellness Tracking

Users can log daily wellness indicators including:

* Symptoms
* Mood
* Energy levels
* Cravings
* Menstrual activity

Historical records are stored and organized to help users observe long-term trends.

---

### 📊 Pattern Visualization Dashboard

Interactive visualizations allow users to explore symptom history and identify recurring wellness patterns over time.

The goal is to transform raw tracking data into meaningful insights rather than isolated daily entries.

---

### ❓ Educational Symptom Guidance

The "Is This Normal?" feature provides educational guidance for common menstrual and PCOS-related concerns.

This feature is intentionally informational and designed to encourage awareness rather than provide medical diagnoses.

---

### 🌸 PCOS-Aware Cycle Education

Traditional menstrual education often assumes regular cycle behavior.

Hormone Harmony provides cycle-phase explanations that specifically discuss:

* Menstrual Phase
* Follicular Phase
* Ovulation
* Luteal Phase

and explains how PCOS may alter or disrupt each phase through irregular ovulation, prolonged cycles, hormonal imbalance, and cycle variability.

---

### 📚 Period Myths & Facts Hub

An India-focused educational module that addresses common misconceptions around:

* Menstruation
* Fertility
* PCOS
* Body image
* Women's health

The feature promotes evidence-based awareness and stigma reduction.

---

### 🤝 Understanding PCOS: A Guide for Men & Boys

PCOS impacts not only individuals but also their families, partners, friends, and support systems.

This module was created to help boys and men better understand:

* What PCOS is
* Common misconceptions
* Emotional challenges faced by women with PCOS
* Practical ways to provide support

---

### 🍱 PCOS Meal Guide

A curated educational resource featuring PCOS-conscious meal ideas and dietary awareness content.

The goal is not to prescribe diets, but to improve nutritional awareness and encourage informed choices.

---

### 🌿 Self-Care & Lifestyle Support

Provides practical guidance around:

* Sleep habits
* Stress management
* Movement and activity
* Lifestyle consistency
* Wellness routines

to encourage sustainable and realistic self-care practices.

## Design Philosophy

Hormone Harmony was built around three principles:

### Education Before Prediction

The platform focuses on helping users understand their experiences instead of generating unsupported health predictions.

### Awareness Before Assumptions

Features are designed to provide context, guidance, and learning rather than making medical claims.

### Support Beyond Tracking

The project extends beyond symptom logging by incorporating education, myth-busting, lifestyle support, and awareness resources that address the broader PCOS experience.
